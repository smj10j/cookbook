ingredients:
  - 1½ oz vodka
  - 1 oz coffee liqueur
  - 1 oz heavy cream
  - ½ oz caramel sauce
  - Ice
  - Flaky salt, for garnish
steps:
  - Drizzle the caramel sauce inside a rocks glass and fill with ice.
  - Add the vodka and coffee liqueur and stir.
  - Float the cream slowly over the top, pouring over the back of a spoon.
  - Finish with a pinch of flaky salt.
tips:
  - Float the cream over the back of a spoon for the classic layered look, then stir before sipping.
  - The pinch of flaky salt on top is what tips it from sweet to crave-able — don't skip it.
  - Warm the caramel slightly so it stirs in smoothly instead of clumping.
  - Stephen's pour — less caramel and more coffee liqueur for a drier, more bitter cup.
