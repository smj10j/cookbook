title: Coconut Pineapple Swizzle

pitch: >-
  A swizzle is the lazy bartender's secret weapon — no shaking, just crushed ice and a spinning
  stick until the glass frosts white. Light rum, pineapple, and a small whisper of coconut cream
  go in together, swizzled until everything chills and dilutes into something soft and tropical
  but never thick. Mound more crushed ice on top, float a few dashes of Angostura across the
  surface, and tuck in a slapped mint sprig so the first sip is herb and bitters before the fruit.
  *Coconut cream is a seasoning here, not the main event — a half ounce reads silky, an ounce reads piña colada.*
serves: 1

  prep: 5
  total: 5

glass: Collins
strength: sessionable

tags:
  - tropical

ingredients:
  - 2 oz light rum
  - 1 oz pineapple juice
  - ½ oz coconut cream (the thick cream layer, e.g. Coco López)
  - ¾ oz fresh lime juice
  - ¼ oz simple syrup
  - 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
  - Crushed ice
  - Mint sprig and lime wheel, for garnish
steps:
  - Fill a Collins or highball glass halfway with crushed ice.
  - Add everything except the bitters.
  - Swizzle with a swizzle stick or a barspoon spun between your palms until the glass frosts.
  - Mound more crushed ice over the top to dome it.
  - Dash the bitters across the top as a float.
  - Slap a mint sprig to wake it up and tuck it in alongside a lime wheel.
tips:
  - Keep it light and barely-creamy, never thick — if it tastes like a piña colada you've overdone the coconut cream.
  - No ice crusher? Wrap cubes in a clean towel and roll a rolling pin over them, or pulse briefly in a blender.
  - Coconut cream clumps when cold, so warm and stir the can until smooth before measuring, and it swizzles in clean.
extras:

    note: A second slapped mint sprig (or a basil leaf) on top doubles the herbal hit and looks the part.
  - label: Make it a pitcher
    note: Batchable — combine everything but the bitters and crushed ice ahead, then swizzle each glass to order so the dilution stays right.

The gentle, beach-chair cousin of the daiquiri — all crushed ice, soft coconut, and a bitters float you smell before you taste.
