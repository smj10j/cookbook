ingredients:
  - 1 oz dark rum
  - 1 oz coffee liqueur
  - ½ banana
  - 1 oz heavy cream
  - ½ oz chocolate syrup
  - 1 cup ice
  - Whipped cream, for garnish
  - Banana slice, for garnish
steps:
  - Add the rum, coffee liqueur, banana, cream, chocolate syrup, and ice to a blender.
  - Blend until thick and smooth.
  - Pour into a hurricane glass.
  - Top with whipped cream and a banana slice.
tips:
  - A very ripe, spotty banana blends sweeter and smoother — freeze the chunks first for an even thicker shake.
  - Drizzle chocolate syrup inside the glass before pouring for a pretty swirl.
  - Balance the sweetness with a slightly bitter coffee liqueur (Mr Black) if you have it.
  - Lauren's pour — a little more chocolate; Pam's — bump the rum to 1.5 oz.

A Jamaican beach-bar staple — frozen, boozy, and basically a banana milkshake for grown-ups.
The coffee liqueur keeps all that sweetness in check.
