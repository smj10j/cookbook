title: Mudslide
slug: mudslide
kind: drink
tagline: A frozen, boozy milkshake of vodka, coffee liqueur, and Irish cream, laced with chocolate.
pitch: >-
  Part cocktail, part dessert, fully unserious — vodka for the kick, Kahlúa for the coffee,
  and Baileys for the cream, all blitzed with ice into a thick, pale-mocha swirl. A ribbon
  of chocolate syrup spiraled down the inside of the glass turns every sip into a stripe of
  fudge, and a tall crown of whipped cream takes it the rest of the way over the top.
  *It's a milkshake that happens to be drunk, and there's no shame in admitting that's the appeal.*
serves: 1
times:
  prep: 5
  total: 5
difficulty: easy
base: vodka
family: frozen
methods:
  - blended
glass: Hurricane
strength: medium
heat: none
tags:
  - creamy
  - dessert
ingredients:
  - 1 oz vodka
  - 1 oz coffee liqueur (such as Kahlúa)
  - 1 oz Baileys Irish Cream
  - 1 cup ice
  - Chocolate syrup, for the glass (optional)
  - Whipped cream, for garnish (optional)
steps:
  - Drizzle chocolate syrup around the inside of the glass for a marbled swirl.
  - Blend the vodka, coffee liqueur, Baileys, and ice until smooth and thick.
  - Pour into the prepared glass.
  - Top with whipped cream and a little more chocolate.
tips:
  - Shake it over ice and skip the blender for a looser, on-the-rocks version.
  - A scoop of vanilla ice cream in the blender turns it into a full-on boozy milkshake.
  - Freeze the glass first so the frozen pour holds its shape instead of melting on contact.
