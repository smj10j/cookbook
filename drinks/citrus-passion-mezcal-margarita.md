pitch: >-
  A margarita built for people who flinch at the word mezcal — here the smoke is a
  seasoning, not the main event. Tart passion fruit and a double hit of lime and lemon
  carry the glass, while a one-ounce pour of mezcal split with blanco tequila leaves only
  a curl of campfire in the background. It comes out tropical and sharp up front, then
  ends with that smoky exhale that makes you go back for another.
  *The mezcal is seasoning, not the star — splitting it with tequila keeps the smoke behind the fruit.*
serves: 1
times:
  prep: 5
  total: 5
difficulty: easy
base: mezcal
family: margarita
methods:
  - shaken
glass: Rocks
strength: medium
heat: none
tags:
  - smoky
  - citrusy
  - tropical
ingredients:
  - 1 oz mezcal
  - 1 oz blanco tequila
  - ¾ oz fresh lime juice
  - ¼ oz fresh lemon juice
  - ¾ oz triple sec
  - ½ oz passion fruit syrup
  - ¼ oz agave syrup (optional, for body)
  - Tajín or sugar-salt, for the rim (optional)
  - Ice
  - Lime wheel, for garnish
steps:
  - Optionally half-rim a rocks glass with Tajín or sugar-salt — a half rim lets you choose chili-lime or clean with each sip.
  - Add the mezcal, tequila, lime juice, lemon juice, triple sec, passion fruit syrup, and agave to a shaker filled with ice.
  - Shake hard for 12–15 seconds, until the shaker frosts over.
  - Strain over fresh ice in the rocks glass.
  - Garnish with a lime wheel.
tips:
  - Passion fruit syrup can be store-bought or homemade — simmer equal parts passion fruit purée and sugar until it dissolves, then cool.
  - Whip-shake (a short, fast shake with just a couple of small ice cubes) for a silkier, more aerated texture.
  - Lean the smoke forward for Stephen by going 1½ oz mezcal to ½ oz tequila; reverse the split for Lauren's lighter, fruitier glass.
extras:

    note: Add a thin serrano slice to the shaker for a tropical-meets-smoky heat that plays well with the passion fruit.
  - label: Make it longer
    note: Drop the spirits to ¾ oz each and top with soda over ice for a smoky passion fruit highball that drinks lower and lasts.
