title: Salty Coconut Honey Rum Smash
slug: salty-coconut-honey-rum-smash
kind: drink
tagline: Gold rum shaken with coconut milk, honey, and a pinch of salt into something silky and sea-breezy.
pitch: >-
  Shake coconut milk hard enough and it stops being a splash and becomes a body — light, silky,
  almost mousse-like — which is the whole trick here. Gold rum and honey syrup bring warmth and
  floral depth, lemon cuts the richness, and a single pinch of flaky salt does what salt always
  does, snapping every flavor into focus. Slap a few mint or basil leaves before they go in,
  shake long over ice, and strain over crushed ice for a drink that tastes like a warm beach at dusk.
  *That pinch of salt is non-negotiable — it turns sweet-and-creamy into crave-able.*
serves: 1
times:
  prep: 5
  total: 5
difficulty: easy
base: rum
family: smash
methods:
  - shaken
glass: Rocks
strength: medium
heat: none
tags:
  - creamy
  - refreshing
ingredients:
  - 2 oz gold rum
  - ¾ oz fresh lemon juice
  - ¾ oz honey syrup (1:1 honey and hot water)
  - 1 oz coconut milk (stir the can first)
  - 1 small pinch flaky sea salt
  - 3–4 fresh mint or basil leaves (optional)
  - Crushed ice
  - Mint sprig, for garnish
steps:
  - Gently slap the herbs between your palms to release their oils and drop them in the shaker.
  - Add the lemon juice, honey syrup, coconut milk, salt, and rum.
  - Shake hard with ice for 15–20 seconds to emulsify the coconut into a light, silky body.
  - Double-strain over crushed ice in a rocks glass.
  - Garnish with a slapped mint sprig.
tips:
  - Make honey syrup at a 1:1 ratio — equal parts honey and hot water, stirred until it pours like simple syrup.
  - Shake longer than feels normal, a full 15–20 seconds, so the coconut milk emulsifies and the drink turns silky instead of separating.
  - Float ½ oz dark rum over the top for a richer, more spirit-forward "Midnight Smash."
