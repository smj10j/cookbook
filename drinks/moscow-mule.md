ingredients:
  - 2 oz vodka
  - ½ oz lime juice
  - 4 oz ginger beer
  - Ice
  - Lime wedge, for garnish
  - Mint sprig, for garnish (optional)
steps:
  - Fill a copper mug (or highball glass) with ice.
  - Add the vodka and lime juice.
  - Top with the ginger beer and stir gently.
  - Garnish with a lime wedge and a sprig of mint.
tips:
  - Spicy ginger beer — not sweet ginger ale — is what makes a mule a mule; the sharper, the better.
  - The copper mug is tradition and keeps it frosty, but a highball glass works fine.
  - Squeeze the lime wedge in before dropping it; the fresh juice brightens the whole drink.
  - Stephen's version — muddle a few slices of jalapeño in first for a spicy mule.
