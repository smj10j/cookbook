ingredients:

  - 1 oz creme de cacao

steps:
  - Shake the crème de menthe, crème de cacao, and cream with ice until cold and frothy.
  - Double-strain into a chilled coupe.
  - Garnish with a mint sprig and chocolate shavings.
tips:
  - Equal parts is the classic build — green crème de menthe for color, white crème de cacao so it stays minty-pale.
  - Shake hard and long; you want it frothy and milkshake-cold.
  - For a richer version, blend it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream instead of the cream.
  - A slapped sprig of garden mint over the top freshens the whole thing.

A New Orleans classic from the 1910s — and the original mint-chocolate dessert in a glass.
Equal parts, shaken cold, and don't skip the chocolate on top.
