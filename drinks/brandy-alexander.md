ingredients:
  - 1½ oz cognac
  - 1 oz creme de cacao
  - 1 oz heavy cream
  - Ice
  - Grated nutmeg, for garnish
steps:
  - Shake the cognac, crème de cacao, and cream with ice until cold and frothy.
  - Double-strain into a chilled coupe.
  - Grate fresh nutmeg over the top.
tips:
  - Shake hard to whip the cream into a silky, frothy texture.
  - Dark crème de cacao gives a deeper chocolate color and flavor; white keeps it paler.
  - Use a decent cognac — it's the backbone, and Pam will taste it.
  - Fresh-grated nutmeg is the finishing aroma; pre-ground won't do it justice.

A 1920s classic and John Lennon's drink of choice — chocolate and cream with a cognac spine.
Equal cream and cacao, a little more brandy, shaken cold.
