tags:

ingredients:
  - 3 oz prosecco
  - 2 oz Aperol
  - 1 oz club soda
  - Ice
  - Orange slice, for garnish
steps:
  - Fill a wine glass with ice.
  - Add the prosecco, then the Aperol, then the splash of soda, in that order so it gently layers.
  - Stir once and garnish with an orange slice.
tips:
  - The order matters — prosecco first, then Aperol, then soda — so it layers instead of going flat.
  - Use lots of ice; a full glass melts slower and keeps the spritz colder.
  - Keep the prosecco brut (dry) — Aperol brings plenty of sweetness on its own.
  - A green olive on the pick is the classic Venetian touch alongside the orange.

The 3-2-1 of the Venetian aperitivo: three parts prosecco, two Aperol, one soda. Sunset in a
glass, and the easiest thing on the whole list to make.
